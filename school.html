Our Rich History

Founded in 2010

Eduverse was founded in 2010 with 40 students and 6 passionate teachers, driven by a mission to nurture excellence and character in young learners.

Growing Community

Today, Eduverse has expanded into a vibrant learning community of 850 students and 50+ experienced teachers, enriched with science labs, ICT centers, and a spacious 12-acre campus.

Academic Excellence

Year after year, Eduverse students excel in national examinations such as WAEC, NECO, and JAMB, with remarkable pass rates and increasing access to university scholarships.
