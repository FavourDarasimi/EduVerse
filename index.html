Our Rich History

Founded in 2010

Eduverse was founded in 2010 with 40 students and 6 passionate teachers, driven by a mission to nurture excellence and character in young learners.

Growing Community

Today, Eduverse has expanded into a vibrant learning community of 850 students and 50+ experienced teachers, enriched with science labs, ICT centers, and a spacious 12-acre campus.

Academic Excellence

Year after year, Eduverse students excel in national examinations such as WAEC, NECO, and JAMB, with remarkable pass rates and increasing access to university scholarships.

Admission Process

1

Enquiry & Tour

Contact us and book a campus tour to learn about our programs, fees, and facilities

2

Get Application Form

Pick up or download the application form; review entry requirements and key dates.

3

Submit Form & Documents

Return the completed form with required documents (birth certificate, recent passport photo, last report/result, transfer letter if applicable).

4

Entrance Assessmentr

Candidate sits a placement/entrance test (Nursery/Primary: literacy & numeracy; Secondary: English, Math, Science).

5

Interview & Review

Meet with the admissions team (parent/guardian + student) to discuss fit and next steps.

6

Offer & Acceptance

Brief: Successful applicants receive an offer letter; accept by paying the acceptance/tuition deposit before the deadline.

7

Enrollment & Onboarding

Complete medical/health form, uniform purchase, and orientation; receive class allocation and start date.
